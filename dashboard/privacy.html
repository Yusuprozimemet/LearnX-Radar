LearnRadar — Privacy Policy

← Dashboard

Last updated: 6 June 2026

LearnRadar is a free daily developer-learning project (a Telegram channel, a podcast feed, and an optional early-access waitlist). This page explains what personal data I handle and your rights under the GDPR.

What I collect

  • Telegram channel (t.me/learnradar): I collect nothing. Telegram manages membership; I never see or store individual subscribers' identities.
  • Podcast feed & dashboard: no personal data.
  • Early-access waitlist form (hosted by Tally): your email address (required), and optionally whether you're an individual or a team and what you'd like to learn. I do not collect names, payment details, or anything else.

Why I use it & lawful basis

I use your waitlist email solely to contact you about early access to personalized lessons. The lawful basis is your consent, given via the form's checkbox. I do not send unrelated marketing.

Who processes it

Form responses are stored by Tally (the form provider). Channel delivery runs through Telegram. I do not sell or share your data with anyone else.

How long I keep it

I keep waitlist emails until early access launches and I've contacted you, or until you ask me to delete them or withdraw consent — whichever comes first.

Your rights

You can ask me to access, correct, or delete your data, or withdraw consent, at any time — just email me and I'll action it promptly. You may also unsubscribe from the Telegram channel at any time by leaving it.
